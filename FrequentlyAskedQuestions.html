Frequently Asked Questions



Are the rooms ensuite?
Yes all our rooms are ensuite with hand basin, toilet & shower

Do you take stag or hen night bookings?
No, we guarantee not to take booking from groups of girls or lads to spoil your stay

What time can we check in?
We will let you check in as soon as the room is ready, normally after 11 am

Can we check in late?
Yes, we are always availible till 11pm but can be later if you let us know your estimated arrival time

What time is check out?
Normally 10am, but at certain times you maybe able to book a late check out, (if room is not booked)

Can we pay on our debit or credit card?
Yes,all major cedit/debit cards accepted (no charge for debit card payments) unfortunatly we have to charge 3% for credit cards, due to the high transaction costs

Can we smoke in our room?
We have smoking rooms,these are on the 2nd floor

Do you take pets?
Small well behaved dogs are welcome,please phone for more imformation

Do you have late keys?
Yes, each adult staying will be given a key on arrival, so that you can come and go as you please

Do you serve evening meals?
No sorry, we stopped this service as there are lots of cafes and takeaways very close.we also own a chip shop, 3 doors from the hotel which gives 10% discount to Maydene guests.

Can I bring takeaways into the hotel?
Yes, you may but we ask you not to take any to the bedrooms as this is not permitted, you may use the dinning room or the bar lounge

Do you have parking?
Yes, we have parking for 4 cars, if you require a space please book, the local streets are also free parking

What time do you serve breakfast?
Breakfast is served between 9am & 9.30am

Do you have a vegetarian option?
Yes we serve a full vegitarian breakfast, also we will do our upmost to meet all your dietry needs

What time does the bar open and close?
We will serve drinks from breakfast till around midnite and beyond, depending on how many people in the bar.

Do we need to bring our own towels?
Towels are suplied in your room but you will need your own for the beach.

Are the rooms cental heated?
Yes all rooms are central heated and are alo fully double glazed.

Are there any metres in our room for electric or tv's?
No, there are no metres in the hotel.

Do you open for christmas and new year?
We don't open for christmas but we do for new year

How far from the beach are you?
We are 30metres from the new south promenade

Do you serve tea and cofee in the bar?
We serve tea and coffee all day.

Do you have entertainment in the hotel?
We are licensed for karaoke but only till midnite
